ABOUT

ALOHA!

Hey there! I'm Perseasis, a freelance illustrator and concept artist who's passionate about character design. I'm all about building a community that creates fascinating worlds and stories together. As a Pacific islander artist, I love infusing my culture and interests into my work. I'm currently a Junior in college, studying fine arts with a focus on Design and Marketing. I'm always eager to learn and share my knowledge with the creative community. When I'm not, you can find me playing games like Dungeon and Dragons, Hades, and Persona 5, exploring new stories and adventures that bring happiness to the world!

PASSION IN DESIGN

I’m all about transforming ideas into visually engaging designs that not only look great but also tell a compelling story. Whether it’s creating captivating logos, designing dynamic marketing materials, or crafting immersive illustrations, my goal is to bring your vision to life in the most creative way possible. As someone who thrives on exploring new concepts, I’m always looking for opportunities to collaborate, learn, and grow.
